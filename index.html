About Me

Hi. I'm Chris. I'm an engineer and programmer in Boulder Colorado. I am currently the Engineering Manager at SparkFun Electronics.

I build, modify, destroy, write, fix, break, design, offend, defend, produce, finesse, force, create, and many other constructive and destructive verbs. I also enjoy bicycling.

I specialize in loud and dangerous electronics, but I've been accused of designing some elegant and useful things as well. Check out my projects for a selection of my work, or poke around in my code sandbox.

If you'd like to converse, geek out, or hear my opinions on Finland (it's great), feel free to get in touch at chris@electronicsforsinners.com.
