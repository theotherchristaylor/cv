About Me

I'm Chris. I'm a project manager and engineer in Boulder, Colorado. I am currently the Engineering Manager at SparkFun Electronics.

I've dedicated my career and personal life to defending and offending the world of DIY electronics. I also enjoy bicycling.

I have a passion for the subversive and dangerous side of electronics, but I've been accused of creating some elegant and useful things as well. Check out my projects for a selection of my work, or poke around in my code sandbox.

If this page took a second to load, that's because it's being served from a Raspberry Pi in my broom closet.

If you'd like to converse, geek out, or hear my opinions on Finland (it's great), feel free to get in touch at theotherchristaylor@gmail.com.
